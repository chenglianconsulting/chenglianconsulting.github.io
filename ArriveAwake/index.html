Why ArriveAwake?

Signal Loss Failsafe

Most GPS alarms fail in tunnels or subways. ArriveAwake detects signature signal loss patterns and triggers a safety alarm if you haven't moved, ensuring you never sleep through your stop in the deep.

Silent Switch Bypass

Never miss an alert because you forgot to toggle your physical switch. ArriveAwake pipes audio through your system's media channel to bypass the silent switch for critical alerts.

Adaptive Profiles

Standard commute? Use a gentle chime. Deep sleep on a long-distance train? Set a location-specific "Extreme" profile to ensure you wake up.

Critical Setup Guide

For ArriveAwake to monitor your location while your phone is in your pocket, please ensure these settings are enabled:

  • 1
    Location Access: "Always"

    Go to Settings > ArriveAwake > Location. Choose "Always". This allows the app to wake you up even when it's in the background.

  • 2
    Precise Location: "On"

    In the same Location menu, verify "Precise Location" is enabled. This is critical for detecting stops that are close together.

  • 3
    Background App Refresh

    Ensure this is enabled in Settings > ArriveAwake so the app can continue monitoring your transit progress.

Managing Locations

  • Adding a Destination

    Tap the "+" button, search for your stop, and set your desired alarm radius. Smaller for tight city stops, larger for long-distance arrivals.

  • Quick Reordering

    Drag and drop your destinations on the home screen to organize your daily route. Tap the toggle to arm an alarm before you start your journey.
