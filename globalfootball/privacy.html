Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Global Football Livescore 2026 ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and discloses your information. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring a safe, transparent mobile experience.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a high-quality experience, the App collects and processes the following limited information:

  • Personalization Choices: We save your selected favorite teams locally on your device using Standard iOS UserDefaults cache keys. This configuration is stored locally and is not sent to our servers.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you grant permission for alerts, we register a device token with Apple Push Notification services (APNs) to schedule match kickoff alerts. We do not store or associate this token with any personal identities.
  • Purchase History & Entitlements: We use Apple's secure StoreKit 2 APIs to process the $0.99 Premium upgrade. We do not collect billing names, credit card details, or addresses; all financial transaction processes are securely managed by Apple.

2. Data Collection by Third Parties

The App fetches live match data from our caching proxy worker hosted on Cloudflare. The request contains an anonymous application header key and does not transmit any device IDs, IP addresses, geographical coordinates, or personal identifiers. The worker does not collect logs containing identifiable user traffic.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected configurations solely to:

  • Display customized schedules based on your favorite teams selection.
  • Deliver local notifications 15 minutes before matches kickoff.
  • Verify and grant access to the live scores premium features.

4. Data Sharing & Retention

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with third-party advertisers or brokers. All configurations are stored locally on your device. Clearing your app cache or uninstalling the app permanently erases this data from your storage.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at legal@chenglianconsulting.com.
