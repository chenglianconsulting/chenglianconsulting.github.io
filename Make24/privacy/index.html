Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Chenglian Consulting, we believe that your privacy is a fundamental right. We have built the **Make 24** iOS and Web applications with a privacy-first approach. Our core philosophy is simple: we do not want your data, and we do not collect it.

Data Collection and Usage

The Make 24 iOS App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • No Accounts: You are never required to create an account or provide an email address to play the game.
  • No Analytics: We do not embed hidden tracking pixels, crash reporters, or product analytics tools into the native iOS app.
  • Local Storage Only: All of your game history, statistics, and high scores are saved exclusively on your local device's secure storage. We have absolutely no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

Internet Permissions

The native iOS app is entirely self-contained and does not require an active internet connection to function.

Note for Web Users: If you are playing the game via our website (make24.chenglianconsulting.com), standard web traffic logs (like IP addresses) may be collected by our hosting provider (GitHub/Netlify) for security and routing purposes. We do not use these logs for tracking.

Children's Privacy

Because Make 24 is a mathematics and logic game, it is suitable for all ages. Because we collect absolutely zero personal information, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and international equivalents.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever decide to introduce features that require data collection (such as cloud syncing or online leaderboards), we will update this policy and explicitly request your opt-in permission before any data is gathered.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the game in general, please contact us at info@chenglianconsulting.com.
